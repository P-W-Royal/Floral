About Us

We make people smile

At Flora boutique we know that sending flowers conveys a truly heartfelt message to someone special. It could be an expression of love, affection, health, happiness and a lot more.

We are proud to help our clients celebrate life by translating their messages into the beautiful language of fresh flowers. That is the goal that everyone in our team dedicates themselves to: Sharing smiles.

what say our clients


Why should I choose Floral boutique for a Pakistan flower delivery?

There is a lot of choice of online flower delivery services on the Internet. We offer a flexible service that takes the complexity out of international flower delivery. With a network of expert florists in many countries around the world as well as our centres In Europe, the US and around the world, we are able to offer that rare mix of the best quality flowers with an easy and fast delivery system. If you want more than just flowers we also have an extensive range of gifts and add-ons to make your order even better.



How to Deliver Flowers to Pakistan?

Delivering flowers to Pakistan with FloraQueen is as easy as any other kind of online shopping! All you need to do is check out our extensive online boutique to find the flowers that you want. Once you’ve selected the perfect flowers, all you need is to follow our easy ordering process. Within just a few clicks, your flowers are on their way to the recipient. Just provide your delivery address, delivery date, and you can even add a free message to go with your bouquet. If that’s not enough, we even provide a number of unique add-ons to make your gift extra special. Pick out chocolates, cards, vases, or cute teddies to add to your flowers.


Can i order by telephone or email?

The safest and most efficient way to order is via our website, for this reason we do not accept orders by email or telephone.



Do you offer same day delivery?

Yes, we work directly with local florists in Pakistan, just around the corner from the delivery address. We can accept same day delivery almost everywhere. The cut off time for same day delivery does vary for each country and time zone, and you will be advised during the buying process if it is not possible. In this case delivery date will not be available in the calendar.



Can I send flowers to Pakistan with Floralboutique?

Yes, we do send flowers to Pakistan. You can also attach a greeting card to the bouquet!


Are PO Boxes Allowed?

We want to ensure that your flowers arrive looking fresh and picture-perfect. Since there’s no telling when someone is going to check their PO Box, we cannot deliver to them. If you provide a PO Box during your order process, an agent contacts you to get a physical delivery address instead.




Do the flowers come with the vase?

A vase is not included in the price unless specified in the product description. If you would like your flowers to be delivered in a vase please make sure to add the product in a second step the buying process.




Can I send the flowers to po box?

Unfortunately not, in this case our agents will ask you to provide a physical address in order to complete your delivery.
